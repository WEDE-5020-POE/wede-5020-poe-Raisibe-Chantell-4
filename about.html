It is more than a place to stay

Since 2012 Ronald McDonald House Charities South Africa has been a home-away-from-home for families whose children are receiving specialised medical treatment. We provide a safe and supportive space where families can rest, share meals, and stay close to the care their children need. Our work goes beyond accommodation. We focus on building a caring community that eases the emotional and financial pressure of having a child in hospital. Key Partnerships McDonalds SA (founding and sustaining partner) Government health departments Hospital networks (e.g., Chris Hani Baragwanath Academic Hospital) Corporate sponsors and community donors (Kuipers Group, Digistics, McCain, Rentokil, Twinsaver etc.) Strategic Goals Strengthen brand positioning as a leader in family-centred healthcare support. Drive awareness and participation in fundraising initiatives. Showcase measurable impact through data, storytelling, and stakeholder engagement. Expand programme reach and multi-sector collaborations. About Keeping families close Ronald McDonald House Charities South Africa is an independent non-profit organisation governed by a Board of Directors and supported by dedicated staff and volunteers. Established in 2012, we are part of a global network of chapters that keep families close to each other and to the medical care their children receive through programs like the Ronald McDonald House and the Ronald McDonald Family Room. More than 94 percent of hospital partners agree that RMHC helps reduce stress and financial burden for families while improving their ability to focus on their childs recovery. Our partnership with hospitals such as the Nelson Mandela Childrens Hospital and the Chris Hani Baragwanath Academic Hospital allows us to bring care and comfort closer to where it matters most
